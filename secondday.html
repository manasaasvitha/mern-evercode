Types of Coffee

Irish Coffee:
A cocktail made with hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and a layer of cream on top.

Espresso:
A strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water under pressure.

Latte:
A creamy blend of espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foam.

Cappuccino:
Similar to a latte but with a greater proportion of milk foam, creating a light texture.

Americano:
Espresso diluted with hot water, resulting in a milder flavor.
